Coffee Selection

Latte – smooth espresso mixed with steamed milk.

Americano – espresso diluted with hot water.

Cappuccino – equal parts espresso, milk, and foam.

Mocha – espresso blended with chocolate and steamed milk.

Espresso – rich, concentrated coffee shot.

Macchiato – espresso marked with a touch of foam.

Flat White – velvety steamed milk poured over espresso.

Cold Brew – slow-steeped coffee served chilled.

Frappe – blended iced coffee with foam.

Affogato – vanilla ice cream drowned in hot espresso.
